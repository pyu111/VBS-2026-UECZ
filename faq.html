Frequently Asked Questions

❓What is Vacation Bible School?

Vacation Bible School (VBS) is a fun and engaging program where children learn about God through Bible stories, songs, games, crafts, and activities in a joyful environment.

🎪 What is the theme this year?

This year’s theme is “Step Right Up!” – a fun circus-themed adventure where kids discover God’s love in exciting and creative ways!

📖 What will kids learn from the Bible?

Children will learn important Bible lessons about God’s love, kindness, forgiveness, and how to follow Jesus in their daily lives.

📅 When and where will the VBS take place?

May 25–29, 1:00 PM – 5:00 PM at United Evangelical Church of Zamboanga Nuñez Ext. St, Zamboanga City, Zamboanga del Sur.

💰 Is there a registration fee?

No, the program is completely FREE.

🎒 What should my child bring?

  • A water bottle
  • Extra clothes
  • A Bible (if they have one)
  • A positive attitude ready to learn and have fun!

🍪 Will snacks be provided?

Yes, snacks will be provided during the program.

📝 How do I register my child?

Parents can register online through the registration form on the main page or register at the church.
